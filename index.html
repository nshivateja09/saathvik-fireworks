Celebrate Responsibly

👨‍👩‍👧

Adult Supervision

Never allow children to handle or light fireworks without close adult supervision.

💧

Keep Water Nearby

Always have a bucket of water or a fire extinguisher ready for emergencies.

↔️

Maintain Safe Distance

Light fireworks in an open area, far from buildings, vehicles, and flammable materials.

🚫

Never Re-light Duds

If a firework doesn't ignite, do not try to re-light it. Wait 20 minutes and then soak it in water.
